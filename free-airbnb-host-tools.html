Free Airbnb Host Tools and Checklists

New and small hosts do not need a complicated software stack on day one. Start by reducing repeated questions, tightening turnovers and making essential information easy to find.

01 Guest welcome book

Put Wi-Fi, check-in, checkout, house rules, appliance notes, parking and local tips in one place.

Get the starter template
02 Welcome message templates

Prepare messages for booking confirmation, one week before arrival, day-of-arrival, mid-stay and checkout.

03 Cleaning turnover checklist

Track linens, towels, trash, bathrooms, kitchen, supplies, damage notes and photo proof before the next guest.

04 Pricing and discount notes

Record weekly/monthly discounts, gap-night offers, last-minute discounts and seasonal events before changing rates.

05 Local guide basics

Guests ask fewer questions when coffee, groceries, transport, attractions and emergency contacts are easy to scan.

06 Direct booking readiness

Before thinking about a direct booking site, make sure the guest experience, reviews and house information are consistent.
